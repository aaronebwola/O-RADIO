About Us

O Radio Xtra is a fully-fledged internet radio station offering captivating content to the Luo-speaking audience primarily from Northern Uganda. Our platform caters to Luo people worldwide, accessible via the web and smartphone applications, broadcasting exclusively in the Luo language.

Our program format is designed to be informative, straightforward, enjoyable, and interactive, reflecting the identity of the Luo community. Our audience can expect a diverse range of content, including informative talk shows, news updates, sports coverage, music mixes, and entertaining segments, all delivered in Luo.

O Radio celebrates the rich culture and values of the Luo people, incorporating strong personalities and offering an eclectic mix of content that promises to transform lives, educate, inform, and entertain.
